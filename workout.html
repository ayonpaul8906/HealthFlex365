Strength Exercises

Push-ups

A great upper body exercise that works your chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Squats

Squats build lower body strength, targeting quads, hamstrings, and glutes.

Plank

Plank strengthens your core, improves stability, and builds endurance with every hold.

Pull-ups

Pull-ups are perfect for building your back and biceps.

Cardio Exercises

Running

A great cardiovascular exercise to burn calories and improve heart health.

Cycling

Cycling is excellent for leg strength and cardiovascular endurance.

Jumping Jacks

Jumping jacks boost endurance, burn calories, and energize your whole body.

Rowing

Rowing targets multiple muscle groups while boosting cardiovascular endurance.

Flexibility Exercises

Hamstring Stretch

Improves flexibility and reduces tension in your hamstrings.

Shoulder Stretch

Helps improve shoulder flexibility and range of motion.

Hip Flexor Stretch

Increases flexibility in your hips and reduces lower back tension.

Child's Pose

A calming pose that stretches the back and helps with flexibility.
